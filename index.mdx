# Welcome to Macru

Macru is a private, secure, and modular AI-powered assistant designed to be a central knowledge engine for an individual's work-related data. It tackles the fragmentation of information across various documents, platforms, and apps by providing a unified, intelligent, and private way to access, query, and interact with your data.

**Core Principles:**
*   **Privacy First:** Your data stays under your control.
*   **Unified Access:** Query all connected sources through a single interface.
*   **Intelligence:** Leverage LLMs to understand context and synthesize information.
*   **Modularity:** Easily extend Macru with new data connectors and capabilities.

This documentation will guide you through setting up, using, and understanding the architecture of Macru.

**Navigate through the documentation:**

*   **[Getting Started](GETTING_STARTED.mdx)**: Learn how to install and configure Macru.
*   **[Features](FEATURES/)**: Explore the core features of Macru in detail.
